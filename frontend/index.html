Principal's Message


DR V. S. Purani | Ph. D. (Civil Engineering)

Dear Students, Faculty and Staff,

Greetings!

As we step into the year 2025, I extend my heartfelt wishes for a year filled with growth, learning, and success. We are living in a fast-paced, knowledge-driven era where advancements in technology are reshaping the world around us at an unprecedented pace. The half-life of knowledge is shrinking, especially in the engineering domain, which evolves moment by moment.

To keep pace with these rapid changes and rising expectations, we must adopt a progressive approach by updating our knowledge standards, formative evaluation methods, learning strategies, and integration of AI and ICT in our education. Lifelong learning and adaptability have become the milestones of success. At the same time, we must adopt a holistic approach today that integrates value-based learning, morals, ethics and the wisdom of the Indian Knowledge System as a commitment to shaping a better tomorrow.

Our institute stands as a platform empowering students and educators to succeed in this dynamic environment. With innovation, values, and collaboration at its core, our campus believes in ‘Aspire to Inspire’ and influences everyone to face challenges, contribute meaningfully to society, and lead with integrity.

Let us embrace this New Year with enthusiasm and a shared vision for excellence and progress. Wishing you all a prosperous and inspiring 2025!

Best Wishes,
Principal
